Hashes

1Take an arbitrary amount of bytes and map them into a fixed size amount of bytes.

2Use a one-way function: simple to compute, difficult to reverse.

3Ideally it is also injective: mapping each valid input to a distinct hash value.

Trojan + Rootkit + Backdoor

You can deploy a hidden virus—Trojan Horse—which hides itself from the user by becoming root—via a Rootkit—and lets attackers into the system bypassing login—opening a Backdoor.

Used in major botnets, targeted hacking attacks, most major hacking incidents, government cyber warfare.

Stuxnet was a worm with a rootkit designed to damage centrifuges built by Seimens.

Most experts believe Stuxnet was developed and deployed by covert US and Isreali military organizations against Iran.
